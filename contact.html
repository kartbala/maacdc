Attend a meeting

Meetings are public, the second Wednesday of each month, with a dial-in option.

Meeting details →

Join the mailing list

Meeting invites, agendas, and accessibility-accommodation notices go out to our list ahead of each monthly meeting. The list reaches dozens of disability-advocacy organizations, District agencies, and community members.

To be added, email info@maacdc.org with the subject “Add me to the MAAC mailing list.”

A note on accessibility

Tell us what you need

If you need this information in another format, or an accommodation to take part in a meeting — ASL interpretation, materials in advance, or anything else — let us know at info@maacdc.org and we’ll make it work. Accessibility isn’t a special request here; it’s the whole point.
