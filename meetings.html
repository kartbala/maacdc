MAAC meetings are open to the public. Come listen, or ask to be added to the agenda.

Access for all

Accommodations

♿

ASL interpretation

American Sign Language interpreters are provided at our meetings, arranged through the DC Office of Disability Rights.

✆

Join by phone

Every meeting has a dial-in number, so you can take part without video or an internet connection.

✉

Request what you need

Need another accommodation? Email info@maacdc.org ahead of the meeting and we’ll arrange it.

Get on the agenda

Want to present?

MAAC regularly hosts District and regional agency leaders — DDOT, DFHV, DPW, and WMATA among them — as well as community advocates and researchers. If you have an issue, a project, or testimony you’d like the Council to hear, reach out and we’ll find a month.
